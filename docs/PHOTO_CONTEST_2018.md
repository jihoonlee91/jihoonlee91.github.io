# 2018 Asia Culture International Photo Contest

## Award record

- Contest: Asia Culture International Photo Contest (아시아문화국제사진공모전)
- Result: Selected work / 입선
- Work: *Gazes of India* (인도의 시선)
- Format: Seven-image series
- Photographer: Jihoon Lee
- Announcement: 2018
- Exhibition: December 11–25, 2018, Asia Culture Center, Gwangju
- Competition scale: 1,063 submissions over 13 days, as stated in the official results announcement

## Work metadata

- Photographed: February 2017
- Locations: Delhi, Varanasi, and Kolkata, India
- Medium: Color photography
- Camera: Canon EOS 6D

## Artist statement — summary

The series records brief encounters with people and places during a journey through India. It began with the direct gazes exchanged between the photographer and people he met, then expanded into a study of everyday movement, curiosity, hospitality, tension, and the small fragments through which a traveler experiences a vast country.
