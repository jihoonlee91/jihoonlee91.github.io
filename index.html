I build AI systems that help semiconductor process engineers make faster and more reliable decisions on process conditions by analyzing multi-source manufacturing data and connecting model outputs to real engineering workflows. My work focuses on advanced memory manufacturing environments, including V-NAND, DRAM-related processes, and HARC etch applications. I own the end-to-end development of production AI decision-support systems — problem formulation, data design, modeling, optimization, recommendation, validation, deployment, monitoring, and workflow integration — supporting process optimization, DOE planning, specification improvement, defect reduction, and recipe-level engineering decisions.
