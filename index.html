Engineer at the intersection of Aerospace GNC, UAV Control, and Semiconductor / Industrial AI

Samsung Electronics — Staff Engineer

📍 Seoul, Republic of Korea · ✉ jihoon91.lee@gmail.com

I work at the intersection of aerospace guidance-navigation-control, UAV flight control, and industrial AI for semiconductor manufacturing. My PhD research at Seoul National University's Flight Dynamics and Control Lab covered morphing-wing UAV control, guidance algorithms, and autonomous carrier landing systems. I now apply that same control-and-optimization mindset, combined with software engineering and machine learning, to build AI systems that help process engineers make faster and more reliable decisions in advanced memory manufacturing (V-NAND, DRAM, HARC etch). I own the end-to-end development of production AI decision-support systems — problem formulation, data design, modeling, optimization, recommendation, validation, deployment, monitoring, and workflow integration — supporting process optimization, DOE planning, specification improvement, defect reduction, and recipe-level engineering decisions.
